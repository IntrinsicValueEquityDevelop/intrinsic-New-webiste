Frequently Asked Questions.

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Reach out to us at info@intrinsicvalueequity.in

What is the investing approach.

As a value investors, we look for the opportunities when great businesses go through rough phase. so whenever businesses goes through such period we buy stakes in them at their min intrinsic Value and when they do better and market understands their worth, we sell them at higher valuations, near their Maximum Intrinsic Value. (Refer BLOG LINK to understand the concept of Minimum/Maximum Intrinsic Value)

What do we mean by long term

Our average holding period is 2.5 yrs, it can vary from 2 to 5 years for individual investments. If your holding period is above 2.5 years then only you should opt for the service

What is the difference in all three listed service

It primarily depends on the capital, If your capital exceeds 10 Lacs over the year, HNI service is recommended for you, For Institutes and family offices having over 5 Crore capital. Enterprize is Must. Enterprize is a focused service only designed for Ultra Large capital investors. where the Model portfolio is maintained for each investor separately. Book a call to know more about Enterprise. Meanwhile HNI is one to many information service which is provided on our own App "Intrinsic Value Equity"

What can one expect from the recommendation service?

  1. Total 20-22 stock recommendation
  2. Research reports (with Buying price, %Allocation, and actionable insights)
  3. Quarterly newsletters
  4. One video meet every quarter
  5. 0-4 no of Dark horses, (High risk – High Reward Stocks)
  6. 1 stock of the month ( 7-8 every year) from Large cap.
  7. Peaceful long term behavior through thick and thin of market.
  8. Exit Recommendations when the price reaches the Target.
  9. Stock picks based on their potential of becoming the multibagger over the next 3-5 years.

What should not be expected from the service?

  1. Short term/F&O calls/Momentum/Swing trading
  2. PMS / profit sharing kind of system.
  3. Money management from our side.
  4. Crypto/NFT or other unrecognized assets related advice.
  5. If your investment horizon is less than 2-5 years, Plz do not Join.
  6. Daily/weekly video calls or Meets
