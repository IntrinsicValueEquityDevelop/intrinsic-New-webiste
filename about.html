Nikhil Gangil

Founder & Research Analyst

Nikhil Gangil is the Founder of Intrinsic Value Equity Advisors and a long-term practitioner of value investing, with over 13 years of experience in Indian equity markets.

He holds an M.Tech from IIT Madras and has previously worked with reputed oil & gas and EPC organizations such as L&T and Worley. This background has given him hands-on exposure to infrastructure, construction, steel, oil & gas, and marine-linked businesses, particularly cyclical industries.

This experience enables him to understand how business cycles evolve and how long-term value is created across different economic phases.

Nikhil's investing philosophy is built on the belief that value and growth are not opposites, but complements. As he often emphasizes, "A value investor should not underestimate growth, and a growth investor should not underestimate value."

His core strength lies in identifying well-run businesses with sustainable growth potential, available at reasonable valuations, while maintaining a strong focus on risk management and capital protection.

Nikhil believes that if downside risks are controlled and permanent capital loss is avoided, long-term compounding and superior CAGR outcomes emerge naturally over time.

His approach to investing can be summarized in a simple philosophy:

Buying peace in chaos is value investing.
